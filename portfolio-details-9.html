Interactive Web Application Development

Designed and developed a vibrant, child-friendly website for St. Francis Nursery and Primary School that effectively showcases the school's educational offerings and values. The website features an intuitive navigation system, colorful and engaging visuals, and essential information sections including academic programs, admission procedures, and school activities. The responsive design ensures seamless viewing across all devices, making it accessible for parents and guardians. Key features include an interactive photo gallery of school events, a user-friendly contact form for inquiries, and regularly updated news sections highlighting student achievements and school events. The website serves as an effective digital platform connecting the school with current and prospective parents while maintaining the warm and nurturing atmosphere that characterizes the institution.
